About Emons

For almost 100 years, Emons has stood for quality, trust and responsibility. With over 120 international locations, we offer you holistic, reliable and innovative logistics solutions — from truck transport, air and sea freight to rail transport and warehouse and distribution logistics.

We rely on cutting-edge technologies, sustainable green logistics concepts and the highest quality standards through continuous monitoring. Thanks to our strong networks in Germany, Europe and worldwide, we ensure that your goods reach their destination reliably and securely. Whether it's general cargo, full load or customs processing — we find the right solution for every challenge, transparently and individually tailored to your needs.
